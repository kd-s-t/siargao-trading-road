# Siargao Trading Road App – Promo Video Script

**Format:** Mobile App Presentation (After Effects style)  
**Length:** ~60–90 seconds  
**Tone:** Clean, modern, island-tech vibe

---

## SCENE 1 – OPENING (0–5s)

**Visual:**  
Cinematic aerial of Siargao roads, waves, and palm trees. Smooth transition into a phone mockup.

**Text on screen:**
```
Trade smarter. Travel better.
```

**SFX:**  
Soft whoosh + ambient island sound

---

## SCENE 2 – PROBLEM (5–12s)

**Visual:**  
Split screens of drivers, locals, and travelers struggling to find services, goods, or fair prices.

**Text on screen:**
```
Finding trusted trades on the road shouldn't be hard.
```

---

## SCENE 3 – INTRO APP (12–20s)

**Visual:**  
App logo animates into the phone. UI lights up.

**Text on screen:**
```
Siargao Trading Road
Your local trading companion
```

## SCENE 4 – FEATURE 1: DISCOVER (20–30s)

**Visual:**  
Scroll through listings (fuel, rides, rentals, goods, services).

**Text on screen:**
```
Discover trades near you
```

**Subtext:**
```
Local • Verified • Real-time
```

## SCENE 5 – FEATURE 2: CONNECT (30–40s)

**Visual:**  
In-app chat, call button, quick deal confirmation.

**Text on screen:**
```
Connect instantly
```

**Subtext:**
```
No middlemen. No hassle.
```

## SCENE 6 – FEATURE 3: TRADE ON THE ROAD (40–55s)

**Visual:**  
Driver accepts a trade, map pins animate along Siargao roads.

**Text on screen:**
```
Trade while you move
```

**Subtext:**
```
Built for life on the road
```

## SCENE 7 – TRUST & SAFETY (55–65s)

**Visual:**  
Ratings, reviews, verified badges animate in.

**Text on screen:**
```
Trade with confidence
```

## SCENE 8 – WHO IT'S FOR (65–75s)

**Visual:**  
Quick cuts: locals, surfers, drivers, small businesses.

**Text on screen:**
```
For locals. For travelers.
For Siargao.
```

**Visual:**  
App Store & Play Store buttons slide in. Sunset road shot in background.

**Text on screen (bold):**

```
Download Siargao Trading Road today
```

**Outro line:**

```
Trade smart. Ride free.
```
